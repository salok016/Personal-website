My Connection to Duluth

I chose Duluth because ever since I was a kid my family has gone camping on the north shore, sometimes at gooseberry, temperance, grand marais, and once on the gunflint trail. This whole area brings me back to when I was a kid and it's also just a beautiful area.

Some of my favorite things about the area are all of the swimming spots. I will never turn down a good cliff jumping trip. I also really like night life.
